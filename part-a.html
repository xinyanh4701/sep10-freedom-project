Software Used Before the Future

Passenger Use

Citymapper

An app used by city commuters all around the world. The app helps the commuters what kind of public transportation they can take based on their destination. The App is available through the Android Playstore, iOS App Store, or their website.

Citi Bike

  • Public bike-sharing system
  • Unlock the bike with a membership or a pass from a bike station
  • If you don't have a membership, you have to pay $3.50 for 30 minutes
  • When you have met your destination, put the bike back in a nearby station

Operator Use

Optibus

  • Use Artificial Intelligence, cloud computing, and advanced optimization algorithms to make mass public transportation better, smarter, and more efficient
  • Used over 500 public transit operations
    • Website Url

Telematics System

  • Use telecommunication and technology
  • Monitor vehicle operations
  • Data is being collected wirelessly
  • Location, speed, time and fuel consumption are being collected
    • Website Url

TripSpark

  • Routing Software
  • In case of an emergency, an emergency detour would be created quickly
  • The system will send up to date information about the arrival and departure of a bus or train to Google
  • Can create schedules for a transit line
  • Help improve the traffic congestion
    • Website Url

Digital Payment

  • Replaces the old traditional payment system
  • More convenient than the traditional
  • More efficient
  • Improved customer experience
