Software Used Before the Future

Passenger Use

Citymapper

An app used by city commuters all around the world. The app helps the commuters what kind of public transportation they can take based on their destination and location of where they are at. The app is available through the Android Playstore, iOS App Store, or the app's website.

Citi Bike

A public bike-sharing system, Citi Bike allows commuters to ride their public bikes at a monthly or one-time cost. According to their website, an annual membership costs $15 a month. A single ride that lasts up to 30 minutes will cost $3.99. Unlimited rides for the whole day will cost you $15. After you are done riding, you just have to put the bike back in one of the thousands of bike station.

Operator Use

Optibus

Optibus, an AI platform that uses artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and advanced optimization algorithms to make mass public transportation better, smarter, and more efficient. Today, the platform is used by more than 500 operators.

Telematics System

The Telematics System uses telecommunication and technology to monitor vehicle operations. Data such as location of the vehicle, the vehicle's speed, time, and energy consumption is being collected wirelessly and sent back to the operator's company for analysis.

TripSpark

TripSpark is a routing software that creates schedules for a transit line. In the case of an emergency, an emergency detour would be created quickly. The system will send up to date information about the arrival and departure of transit vehicle to Google. The routing software also improves traffic congestion.

Digital Payment

The old traditional payment system requires the physical form of money. However, digital payment replaces the old traditional payment system. The digital payment system is more convenient and efficient than the traditional payment system since it requires only a card, smartphone, or any tech product that has the ability to pay, so no need to carry money around.

Hardware Used Before the Future

Cashless Payment System

For the cashless payment system to work, a card or mobile phone must be very close to the contactless payment machine. To make this possible, NFC (near field communication) is required so the machine's screen would display an approval or rejection.

Control Systems

Located inside of the transit vehicle, the control system monitors the vehicle and allows the vehicle to function properly. The control system is an essential component of a vehicle because the vehicle wouldn't even start at the first place.

Automated Stop Announcement Systems

Using display screens or speakers, automated stop announcement systems inform the driver/conductor and passengers what stop they are at.

Traffic/Train Signals

Traffic or Train Signals manage traffic flow between vehicles and people. They also help reduce collisions.

Video Surveillance Cameras

Video Surveillance Cameras record any activity of passengers and the driver/conductor. Video cameras also record activity surrounding people. Cameras protect people because in case of an accident, video surveillance cameras could be used as evidence to find out who the culprit is.
